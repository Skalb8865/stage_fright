Privacy Policy

Introduction

Stage Fright is a fictional band website created for a website design competition hosted by BPA(Business Professionals of America). The website is part of a simulated business project. However, any information collected on this site will be used for competition purposes only and will not be used for real transactions. This Private Policy follows standard procedures inside the industry and demonstrates a professional approach to online privacy management.

Information We Collect

We may collect information such as names, email addresses, browsing activity, and payment details if you have purchased either merchandise, tickets, or a membership.

How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your information to improve our services and keep you updated on new album and tour dates. You will also be notified of changes to our membership (The Amplified Brigade) and the FAQ.

Data Protection

We have implemented many different security measures to protect your infomation/data from unauthorized access(Such as encryption). Your trust is important to us and we will make sure to keep it.

Contact Us

Regarding any questions or information please feel free to contact us at stagefrightrb@gmail.com or contact us through our contact page as we appreciate any feedback or criticisms.
