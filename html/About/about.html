About Us

Stage Fright is a 2022 American rock band formed in Columbus, Ohio. Our band consists of Lead Vocalist Aidien Gooden, Lead Guitarist Sean Kalb, Drummer Jabari Hill, and Lead Bassist Gabe Zeigler. Our debut album, "PROMETHEUS," was released on September 16, 2022 by TNR Records.

Our Members

Aidien Gooden

Aidien is an avid enjoyer of the music arts; back when he was a child, he was inspired by singers like Micheal Jackson, User, and Frank Sinatra. These singers made Aidien realize his passion for singing.

Sean Kalb

Since he was young, Sean has been playing with guitars; his first game was Guitar Hero, and he has fully maxed out the game. Sean joined the band to put his skills to the test

Jabari Hill

Jabari started drumming when he got an opportunity to be his church's drum player. He fell in love with the musical item and has been playing it ever since. His favorite movies are Whiplash and Sound of Metal.

Gabe Zeigler

Gabe has always been interested in the musical arts. From a young age, he learned to play many instruments like piano and guitar, but his true calling was for the bass.

The Beginning

Sean contacted his friends, who shared his passion for experimenting with sound. At first, it was just jam sessions—unpolished, raw, and experimental. They would meet after hours, playing until dawn, weaving together genres and sounds that didn’t quite fit into the mainstream. Their early work was chaotic and dissonant, but there was something undeniable about their energy. Their breakthrough came when they played at a local music festival in early 2022. The crowd was electrified, and a small but devoted fanbase began to take shape.
