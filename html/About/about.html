About Us

Who are Stage Fright

Stage Fright is an American rock band formed in Columbus, Ohio, in 2021. Our band consists of Lead Vocalist Sean Kalb, Lead Guitarist Aidien Gooden, Lead Drummer Jabari Hill, and Lead Bassist Gabe Zeigler. Our debut album, "PROMETHEUS," was released on September 16, 2021, by TNR Records.

About Us

Where it all started

Sean contacted his friends, who shared his passion for experimenting with sound. At first, it was just jam sessions—unpolished, raw, and experimental. They would meet after hours, playing until dawn, weaving together genres and sounds that didn’t quite fit into the mainstream. Their early work was chaotic and dissonant, but there was something undeniable about their energy.
