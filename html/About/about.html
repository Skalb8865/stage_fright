About Us

Stage Fright is a 2022 American rock band formed in Columbus, Ohio. Our band consists of Lead Vocalist Aidien Gooden, Lead Guitarist Sean Kalb, Drummer Jabari Hill, and Lead Bassist Gabe Zeigler. Our debut album, "PROMETHEUS," was released on September 16, 2022 by TR Records.
